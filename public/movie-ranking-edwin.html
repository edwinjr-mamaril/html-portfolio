The Best Movies According to Edwin

My top 3 movies of all time

Penduko

This movie is made from the Philippines starring Matteo Guidicelli as the protagonist. I love this movie because it is filled with extraordinary fight scenes.

Johnny English

Ever since I was a kid, I have been a fan of Rowan Atkinson. This movie is not only jam-packed with comedy but also with action.

Wreck-It Ralph

I enjoyed watching this movie because of the cool animation.
